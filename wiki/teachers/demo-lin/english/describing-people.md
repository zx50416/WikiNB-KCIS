## Learning goals

- Use common adjectives for people
- Write 3–5 sentences about a friend or family member

## Useful words

- Appearance: tall, short, long hair, glasses
- Personality: kind, funny, quiet, hard-working

## Example

My sister is tall and has long hair. She is kind and funny.

## Practice

1. Circle five adjectives you can use today.
2. Write about your best friend (5 sentences).
