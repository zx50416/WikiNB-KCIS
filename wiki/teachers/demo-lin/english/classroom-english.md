## Learning goals

- Ask for help politely in English
- Respond to teacher instructions

## Key phrases

| English | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| May I go to the restroom? | 我可以去洗手間嗎？ |
| How do you spell that? | 怎麼拼？ |
| I don't understand. | 我聽不懂。 |
| Could you repeat that, please? | 可以再說一次嗎？ |

## Practice

1. Role-play: ask to borrow a pencil.
2. Say three classroom phrases to a partner.
