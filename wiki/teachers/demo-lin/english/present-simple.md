## Learning goals

- Use the present simple for habits and facts
- Add **-s / -es** for he / she / it

## Key points

1. I / you / we / they + base verb
2. He / she / it + verb-s
3. Negatives: don't / doesn't + base verb

## Example

- She **plays** tennis every Sunday.
- They **don't** eat meat.

## Practice

1. He (go) _____ to school by bus.
2. Make negative: I like coffee.
