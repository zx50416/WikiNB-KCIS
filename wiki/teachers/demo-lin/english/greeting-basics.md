## Learning goals

- Use common greetings in class and daily life
- Reply politely to “How are you?”

## Key phrases

| English | 中文 |
|---------|------|
| Good morning | 早安 |
| How are you? | 你好嗎？ |
| I'm fine, thank you. | 我很好，謝謝。 |
| Nice to meet you. | 很高興認識你。 |

## Example dialogue

**A:** Good morning! How are you?  
**B:** I'm fine, thank you. And you?  
**A:** Great, thanks!

## Practice

1. 跟旁邊同學用英文互相問好。  
2. 把表格三句話各說兩次。

## Notes

正式場合可用 *Good afternoon* / *Good evening*。這是示範筆記。
