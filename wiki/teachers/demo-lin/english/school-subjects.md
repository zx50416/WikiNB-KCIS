## Learning goals

- Name common school subjects in English
- Say what you like / don't like

## Vocabulary

Math, English, Science, History, Art, Music, PE

## Example

- My favorite subject is **Science**.
- I don't like **History** very much.

## Practice

1. List your timetable in English.
2. Ask a partner: What is your favorite subject?
