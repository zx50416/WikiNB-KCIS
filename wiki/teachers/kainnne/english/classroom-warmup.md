## Learning goals

- Use short classroom English confidently
- Respond to teacher instructions

## Key phrases

1. **Please take out your book.**
2. **May I go to the restroom?**
3. **I don't understand. Can you say it again?**

## Practice

Pair up and act out a 30-second classroom dialogue.
